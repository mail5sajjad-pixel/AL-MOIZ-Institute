AL.Moiz Institute:

-: About :-

AL.Moiz Institute is a professional computer training institute established in 2009 in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. It provides quality education and practical training in Information Technology and computer-related fields. The institute aims to help students develop technical skills and prepare for successful careers in the digital world.


The institute offers a friendly learning environment where students can improve their knowledge through practical work and real-world projects. Experienced instructors guide students and help them build confidence, professionalism, and problem-solving skills. AL.Moiz Institute also encourages internships and hands-on learning to prepare students for future opportunities.


Over the years, AL.Moiz Institute has trained thousands of students and has become a recognized center for IT education. Its mission is to help students learn valuable skills, become self-reliant, and build successful careers in technology and freelancing.
